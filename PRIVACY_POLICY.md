# Privacy Policy for Year Dots

**Last Updated:** February 1, 2026

## Overview

Year Dots is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains our data practices in clear terms.

**The short version:** We don't collect, store, or share any of your data. Period.

## Data Collection

**Year Dots collects ZERO data.**

- ❌ No personal information
- ❌ No usage analytics
- ❌ No crash reports
- ❌ No advertising identifiers
- ❌ No location data
- ❌ No internet connectivity

## Permissions

Year Dots requires only one permission:

### SET_WALLPAPER (Wallpaper Access)
**Purpose:** To set and update your device's wallpaper  
**Usage:** This permission is used exclusively to apply the generated wallpaper to your device  
**Data Storage:** None - the wallpaper is generated on-device and sent directly to the Android system

### Other Permissions
Year Dots does NOT request:
- Internet access
- Location services
- Camera or microphone
- Contacts or calendar
- Storage access (beyond wallpaper setting)

## Local Storage

Year Dots stores your customization preferences (colors, shape, size) locally on your device using Android's encrypted DataStore. This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is only accessible to the app
- Is deleted when you uninstall the app
- Cannot be accessed by other apps

## Third-Party Services

Year Dots uses **zero third-party services**:
- No analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
- No crash reporting tools
- No advertising networks
- No social media integrations
- No cloud services

## Children's Privacy

Year Dots does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for all ages.
